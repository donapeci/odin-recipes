Banana bread recipe

This banana bread is my grandma's masterpiece. It's soft, perfectly moist, and smells like comfort. She always said the secret was using very ripe bananas, "the uglier, the better", she'd laugh.

Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 very ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup butter, preferably unsalted
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • Two small eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cup of self-rising flour

Method

Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). In the meantime, mash the bananas in a large bowl until smooth, then mix them with the melted butter, sugar, and eggs. Add the vanilla extract and slowly incorporate the flour using a sifter to keep the texture light and airy. Pour the batter into a greased loaf pan and bake until golden and fragrant, about 45-50 minutes. Let it cool on a rack (if you can wait that long!) and enjoy a warm slice with a cup of coffee or tea.
