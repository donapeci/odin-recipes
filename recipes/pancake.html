Pancakes

These pancakes were our Sunday morning ritual. My grandma would wake up early, hum a song, and fill the kitchen with the smell of butter and vanilla.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • A pinch of salt
  • Optional: 1/4 of chocolate chips (They will end up amazing)

Method

Whisk all the ingredients together in a large bowl until the batter is completely smooth. Make sure not to overmix, the secret to fluffy pancakes is keeping the batter light and airy. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and melt a small piece of butter to prevent sticking. Pour small circles of batter (about a quarter cup each) onto the pan. When tiny bubbles begin to form on the surface and the edges start to look set, gently flip each pancake using a spatula. Cook for another minute or two until golden brown on both sides. Serve warm with a drizzle of honey, a spoonful of jam, or even fresh fruit and whipped cream, whatever makes you happiest in the morning.
